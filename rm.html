"I want to free myself in order to exist as the Real Me."



Are there moments when you feel as though life is sparkling?

Ah… [with sparkling eyes] I’m not sure how I should interpret the expression “life is sparkling.” If I’m to express it in my own way, I’d say it’s the moments I’m faithful to myself. I believe I’m doing well when I’m making music and sticking to a routine, like maintaining my daily workout schedule. And when I get to meet and talk to good people. You know those moments that fly by so quickly you can’t even spare a second to check the time? It’s those times I feel as if life is sparkling.



When I read the lyrics you’ve written, they often feel like scenery to me. Not just images, but landscapes I can enter.

Painters I like tend to be that way. They allow me to experience a piece with my mind. When you stand before their work, it’s as though you’ve been transported to a particular moment. Their work contains blank spaces. When I keep seeing and pursuing what I like, I find my own work begins to resemble those very things. I train myself in images by trying to express and implement musical textures through a variety of senses.
